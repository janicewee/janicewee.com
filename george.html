George Wong


A Determined and Compassionate Man

George Wong is a prominent character in Janice Wee's novels Singapore's Runaway and Two Worlds, One Love & A Serial Killer. As Amelia's brother and Stanley's stepuncle, George plays a significant role in the lives of the central characters. He is introduced briefly in Singapore's Runaway but takes on a main role as the private detective pursuing the serial killer in Two Worlds, One Love & A Serial Killer.

George is a businessman who handles the Wong trading business. He also works as a private detective, actively fighting against opium addiction and its destruction of society. He investigates a murder case that he suspects is linked to the opium syndicates and eventually solves the crime from within a suspect's household.

George helps Amelia search for her runaway stepson, Stanley, across the islands of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. His primary motivation is to ease his sister's distress and bring Stanley home, even if it means using force. However, as he gets to know Stanley, he develops empathy and understanding for his nephew's situation.
