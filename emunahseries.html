Emunah Chronicles Book 1: Disturbing Dreams

A #Scifi Fantasy Thriller.

Emunah's personal A.I. declares amorous intentions towards her. It wants to enter the physical world in a body cloned from her crush.

While investigating a corporate conspiracy, she comes face to face with an alien that she suspects is stalking her.

The clock is ticking. What will happen to Emunah?

Emunah Novel : The Quest for Immortality.

A #Science fiction and superpowers novel set in the end times.

This novel begins right after Emunah's A.I. hits on her, but from Leo's point of view as he works to protect his friends through the prophesied apocalypse.

Their clique splits up as some vanish, others receive the Antichrist's gifts while others go underground.

Decisions are made, consequences borne. All the way to the epic conclusion of cosmic proportions.

It is a captivating and mind blowing read.

The Beast's Mark

A #scifi horror tale.

This takes place 3 and a half years after Emunah book 1. The Antichrist rules the world and no man can buy or sell anything without the Mark of the Beast. Beast uses deception, Science and the occult to establish his rule. Who can stand up to him and live?

The Characters & Events in The Quest for Immortality

A Companion Guide for the Emunah Series.

This character and event guide came about at the urging of beta readers to provide some sort of character map for the saga. The story covers many themes, through the lives of a large cast of characters, each having his or her own character arc in the tale.

It's written in the style of historical fiction, using prophetic events in the future in place of known events from the past. Instead of history books, I used the Book of Revelation to provide the background and events for the entire saga.
