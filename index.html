Emunah Chronicles Series

It all began when Emunah's AI turned sentient and refused to shut down in Emunah Chronicles Book 1, Disturbing Dreams. In this chilling tale, Emunah's housemates step in. Their investigations draw them into a corporate conspiracy. Their antics gain Emunah an extra terrestrial stalker. It's an eerie tale that could happen in our lifetime.

The story continues in The Beast's Mark, three and a half years after Emunah's disappearance in The Rapture. The Antichrist rules the earth and forces everyone to take the mark of the beast. Emunah's friends who were left behind refuse and go underground, hiding in Leo's underground city.

I originally intended this to be a 7-book series. But after consulting with Dr Peter Wee Huat Leong, a Bible teacher commended to teach in brethren assemblies around the world, I found that I got my timeline and geography messed up. The entire 7 year tribulation is centred around Israel as it is the time of Jacob's trouble. My original plan was to centre it in an unnamed country in the west. The mark of the beast is after the 3 and a half year mark, not immediately after the Rapture. So decided to start from scratch with The Quest for Immortality. This time, I turned to Dr Wee for guidance to get the biblical aspect, timeline and geography correct.

That led to my speculative Science Fiction novel, The Quest for Immortality. You can get the pdf of the entire novel here, for free. Free ebook pdf - The Quest for Immortality.

The Emunah Chronicles Novel : The Quest for Immortality by Janice Wee
Readers found The Quest for Immortality complex with so many themes and characters they asked for a character guide. I decided to expand on the story and turn it into an easy to read 12 book series. The pre-tribulation and tribulation period stories are in the Emunah Short Stories series. It's realistic fiction turned dystopian in books 5 to 8.

The Millennial Kingdom stories take a completely different theme and mood altogether as these are set in a utopian world. This period is told through the eyes of Billy Lionheart, a boy who was born during the tribulation and entered the Millennial Kingdom as a surviving mortal. These stories are lighthearted and fun. Suitable even for upper middle grade reader and up, with a Narnia-like vibe. Check out The Adventures of Billy Lionheart for these fun reads.

The final book is Dragon Unbound. It starts with the Dragon, the great deceiver known also as Satan, bound for a thousand years. Then it tells the story of the utopian rule of the King of Kings seen through the eyes of Billy and Bluma, until the release of Dragon and the chaos he unleashes.

These 12 books cover the entire timeline in The Quest for Immortality, through different points of views, for a rich and fully fleshed out universe.

Happy reading!

Emunah Short Stories: Abigail

Review by Miranda P. YEOH

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

December 10, 2024

Have you noticed how strong relationships, partnerships, and marriages often stem from childhood bonds, school friendships, or church connections? This story introduces Abigail, a relatable heroine who isn’t perfectly organized or blessed with striking beauty. Abigail finds herself drawn to a muscular man with worldly values, overlooking the quiet love and patience of Aaron, her steadfast childhood friend. The story turns darker with Stella, Abigail’s boss, and a Sunday Christian. Stella’s animosity toward Abigail and others leads her down a path of hate, culminating in more sinister acts. It raises thought-provoking questions: Are hatred and murder sins of the same family, although they differ in magnitude? How should a believer respond to such trials? This compelling short story offers valuable lessons about gratitude, faith, and the dangers of taking blessings for granted. It’s a poignant reminder to cherish what we already have. Don’t miss it!
