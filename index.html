Welcome to Janicewee.com

Get behind the scenes into the imaginative mind and life behind these wild tales of exotic worlds and cultures.

My books Singapore's Runaway and Two Worlds, One Love & A Serial Killer tell the tale of a runaway who joins a haunted ship to hide from his family. He hides his past as he adventures with shipmates through islands in Southeast Asia, encounters pirates, betrayal and finds true love, even as his family hunts him down. Will he stay with his sweetheart and start a new life in Indonesia or will he return home with his family in Singapore?

Naughty Little Nonya is a collection of short stories share the shenanigans of a little girl and her cousins in family drama, angst and humour.
