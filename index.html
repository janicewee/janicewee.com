What I'm working on

In my personal opinion, the biggest challenge I have ever faced was to write my novel, The Quest for Immortality, a speculative science fiction book.

It's like historical fiction, but instead of setting the story against the backdrop of historical events, I wanted events that are yet to come - Bible Prophecy.



Now that's even more intimidating to me than history and trust me. I'm no history buff. I went to my late Dad's second brother, Dr. Peter Wee, for advice. He's been a tremendous help - guiding me through the pre-tribulation rapture, premillennial interpretation of Bible prophecy, which I find makes the most sense.

It became a long novel with so many characters, themes and story arcs that beta readers asked for character maps to help them understand the story. On hindsight, the breadth and depth of the story could not be adequately covered in a single book.

That long novel was written for a certain person.
Who?

I have no idea, other than that individual is an intellectual and an atheist.

My next project is to write an entire series of 12 books, set in the end times. This time, I can delve deeper into each character and each story arc - my new series : Emunah Short Stories.

Sweetcorn Suzie
- a collection of short stories.

If you are looking for epic books that wrap truth in fiction, you'll love these stories by fiction author Janice Wee. The best stories have elements of truth in them. Janice has woven family lore, history, office gossip, life's lessons and observations into these heart warming tales.

These range from scary stories to tell in the dark, science fiction and fantasy, bedtime stories, historical fiction, clean romance and children's books. She is a huge fan of comic books, especially of DC characters Superman and Captain Marvel, hence her entire collection of Itty Bitty Billy Batson fan fiction in Wattpad and AO3. The inspiration behind these fan fics are in the Fan Fiction section of this website.

Her latest is a love story: Abigail. This kicks off the 12 book Emunah Short Stories series which is set around the timeline of the Emunah Chronicles. Book 1, Abigail, introduces Ben, Stella and Oliver during the calm before the storm.
