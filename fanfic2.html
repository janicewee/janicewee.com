Billy Batson - A New Adventure Fan Fics

I've run out of good Billy Batson fan fics to read. I've read all I could find on the internet and am hungry for more. So I started writing Billy Batson is Captain Marvel fanfics to satisfy my appetite.

Over time, I realised it's no fun writing if I'm the only one reading my own fics.

My Fan Fics on AO3 My Fan Fics on WattPad

What to write.

There doesn't seem to be much interest in Billy Batson as Captain Marvel out there since the poor reception of Shazam! Fury of the Gods, which I thought was a pretty good movie. Disclaimer: I'm no fan of the New 52 take on Billy which the movie follows. That said, I want more original Captain Marvel fan fics and was wondering whether there's any one out there who'd still be interested in reading these.

So I did this poll.

First, anyone interested in Billy?

Next, what do readers want to read?

I've got a few story arcs I'd like to explore. In between writing my own books, I have time to do just one series/story and asked you guys on AO3 and Tumblr.

From the poll on my Tumblr blog, World of Stories and Dreams (yeah, I know it's a mouthful), an idea reveal where Captain Marvel is really a tiny little boy is the most popular storyline. Delightfulwhispersgalaxy suggested combining the story ideas.

I like that!

The identity reveal would go very well with A villian captures Billy and is humiliated because no one believes that is Cap. I want to thank everyone for your suggestions on AO3 and Tumblr and for taking the time to vote!

Here's the first chapter of my new Billy Batson Fan Fic series - Super Reporters. I'm posting it on my blog first. I'll put it up on AO3 when I've written more chapters and decided how I want to organise it.

Comics Canon:

In Shazam Power of Hope, you'd see a day in the life of Billy Batson. This is the Billy I want to write. His pure character - his selflessness. That's something so lacking in modern day Shazam. Here he brings hope to children in the hospital in spite of his busy schedule.

My Fanfic:

Billy Batson

For my fic, Billy has only been Captain Marvel for less than a year. He's still an itty bitty little kid but with maturity that puts some grown ups to shame. He's nothing like the jerk New 52 depicts Billy to be.
