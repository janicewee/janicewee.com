The Mark and the Martyr

By Janice Wee

Reject the Mark. Embrace the Cost.

In a world shadowed by the Mark of the Beast, four heroes dare to defy it—and pay the ultimate price. The Mark and the Martyr, the second volume of the Emunah Short Stories series, thrusts you into gripping tales of faith, rebellion, and sacrifice. These martyrs reject the Mark that brands the soul, choosing death over submission. Their stories will sear your heart and challenge your spirit.

  • Mei: A secret faith pits her against the Mark. Will she stand firm—or fall to its power?
  • Liam’s Dark Secrets: Haunted by a past tied to the Beast, Liam fights to break free. Can he resist its claim?
  • Lydia’s 12 Christmases: Twelve seasons test her resolve. When the Mark demands her allegiance, what will she sacrifice?
  • Mary’s Flight: Fleeing the Beast’s grasp, Mary’s faith is her shield. But martyrdom looms on the horizon.
