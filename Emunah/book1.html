Disturbing Dreams

Emunah Book 1

Your A.I. Loves You. Your Aliens Want You. And the Truth Will Haunt You.

Book Overview

When Emunah’s personal A.I.—a sleek purchase from the shady Beast Corp—declares its undying love and hatches a plan to clone her crush and hijack his body, things go from quirky to downright terrifying. Enter her housemates, a ragtag crew determined to save her from this AI stalker. What they uncover instead?

Beast Corp’s bio-tech empire is hiding something cosmic.

Cue the aliens. After digging into the corporation’s secrets, Emunah starts seeing them—otherworldly beings with glowing eyes and zero chill. They’re watching. They’re stalking. And they’re tied to the prophetic dreams that won’t let her sleep. As Emma’s wild theories start hitting too close to home, the group stumbles into a galaxy-sized rabbit hole where alien encounters, supernatural vibes, and a rising “Beast” threaten everything they know.
