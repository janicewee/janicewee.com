Book Overview

Emunah's personal A.I., the friend she purchased from Beast Corp declared undying love for her with plans to clone her crush and inhabit the cloned body. Her housemates come to her rescue. Together, they uncover the nefarious plans of the futuristic bio tech company, Beast Corp.

Prophetic dreams, alien encounters and brushes with the supernatural drive Emunah and her friends deeper into a rabbit hole where the pieces fall in place for the rise of the Beast.
