Emunah Chronicles Book 1:
Disturbing Dreams

In a world on the precipice of the unknown, young Emunah finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, technological wonders, and cryptic dreams. As her heart battles with feelings for her enigmatic friend Jayden, Emunah seeks solace and advice from an AI. The response she receives is as chilling as it is unthinkable.

Surrounded by a cast of vibrant characters including Leo, Oliver, Logan, and Ava, Emunah is drawn into the shadows of an insidious corporation - The Beast Corp. The group’s research uncovers unsettling projects involving implantable cell phones and DNA manipulation. It’s a race against time as they begin to see haunting correlations with ancient prophecies.

Emunah’s dreams become more disturbing. Nightmares where ethereal visions and monstrous hybrids collide, whispering of a reality that sends shivers down her spine.

Amidst personal turmoils and earth-shattering revelations, Emunah’s path is forever altered by a tragic loss, shadowy figures, and the allure of a spiritual fellowship led by the mesmerizing Jezebel.

As Emunah delves deeper into the mysterious workings of the Beast Corp, the line between dreams and reality blurs. She must decide who she can trust, how to decipher her disturbing dreams, and what role she is destined to play in events much larger than herself.

“Disturbing Dreams” is an explosive mix of science fiction, suspense, and spiritual intrigue. It’s an electrifying tale that dances on the edge of the apocalypse, asking the reader: Can one person change the tides of fate?

Hold on tight. Once you enter Emunah’s world, there’s no turning back.

Emunah Chronicles Book 2:
The Beast’s Mark

The world is in turmoil as a beguiling figure known as The Beast seizes control, promising an age of prosperity while harboring sinister motives. With the cunning of a sorcerer and the technology to bend reality, The Beast enforces his will through an insidious mark, veiled in promise but fraught with darkness.

Rejoin Emunah’s allies, Logan, Jayden, Ava, and new characters as they are thrust into a whirlwind of deception, prophecy, and rebellion. Logan, tormented by visions of the Four Riders, realizes he may be an essential piece in a game he doesn’t yet understand. Jayden, who’s been building an underground refuge with his friends, finds his heart caught in a war of love and loyalty.

The mark of The Beast, bearing the haunting 666, becomes a symbol of terror and submission. It is a device infused with ominous technology, granting control, and allegiance to The Beast. As humanity buckles under its dominion, those who resist face insurmountable odds.

Ava, a resilient survivor, navigates the treacherous terrain between submission and defiance. When she is faced with the ultimate choice, her path converges with Jayden’s, and together they must fight not only for their own souls but for the soul of the world.

From breathless chases to haunting dreams, from the pulse of romance to the terror of subjugation, “The Beast’s Mark” is a masterful blend of science fiction and spiritual warfare. The fate of humanity hangs in the balance, as characters wrestle with prophecies, heartache, and the ever-encroaching shadow of The Beast.

This heart-pounding sequel will challenge the very essence of what it means to be human, in a world where the line between man and monster is razor-thin.

Witness the rise of the valiant. Experience the terror of The Beast’s Mark.
