Overview

Bound for a thousand years, Dragon, the Great Deceiver, seething with fury, seeks revenge. In a world where lions babysit human infants and immortals hang out with mortals, childhood sweethearts Billy and Bluma forge a family that grows into a nation across centuries. Billy's workaholic tendencies leads to Bluma raising their children on her own, making their sprawling legacy vulnerable to Dragon's cunning attacks. When the Great Deceiver entangles them in tentacles of deceit, who will stand? Who will be dragged into the Lake of Fire?

This is the grand finale in the Emunah Chronicles series by Janice Wee.
