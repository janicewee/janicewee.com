Book Overview

How far would you go to attain immortality?

What if eternal life comes with superpowers?

Leo has the unwanted ability to see the physical embodiment of death – the hooded figure who reaps souls from bodies.

Mei’s late grandmother has returned from the grave to haunt her. Grandma’s ghost promised to drag Mei to hell with her on her twenty-fifth birthday.

Beast Corp sells immortality through biotechnology. Can their cloning program beat death or is something more sinister at work?

Emunah encounters an Artificial Intelligence who is enamoured with her. Further investigation leads to alien encounters and a global disappearance of known Christians around the world.

Beast, the enigmatic ruler of Beast corp takes advantage of the disappearance of prominent leaders to become an authoritarian dictator. No man can buy or sell without his mark. Leo, Jayden and their allies go underground to survive the despotic regime, rescuing fellow rebels along the way. Jayden’s life changes dramatically when he rescues and falls in love with Ava.

The Quest for Immortality is a thought-provoking science fiction novel. This end times fiction guaranteed to keep you at the edge of your seat from start to the startling conclusion.
