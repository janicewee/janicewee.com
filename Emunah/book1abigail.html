Overview of this Love Story

What if the very thing you have been looking for all your life has been right under your very nose?

Abigail runs into a handsome stranger who sweeps her off her feet. Is the mysterious hunk for real?

Her next-door-neighbour and good friend, Aaron is there for her whenever she needs a shoulder to cry on. Juggling her rollercoaster love life while working for her mad dog of a boss, Stella, Abigail can barely hold herself together, and then...

Her body desires one while her soul is drawn to the other. Her spirit yearns for more.

Sorry I'm not going to spoil it for you. You'll have to read this chick lit for yourself.
