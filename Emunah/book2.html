Book Overview

Prominent global leaders have vanished leaving a power vacuum. The enigmatic leader of Beast Corp steps up to unite the world with promises of a global utopia.

Families are shaken with the disappearance of loved ones. The only thing the abducted have in common is that they were all Christians.

Leo, Jayden and those left behind work tirelessly to survive the new regime, even as fellow rebels meet their doom in the hands of Beast's superpowered followers. Who will survive? Who will fall to Jezebel's deadly charms?
