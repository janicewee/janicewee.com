Overview

Post vanishing, the world plunges into lawlessness. A new leader emerges from the chaos, uniting the world behind his sinister agenda. Those who know the truth are slaughtered. Mei, his former employee, flees and joins the rebels as a rescue pilot.

Her previous life managing super-powered clones is a two-edged sword as she and her new friends fight for survival, making their way to the secret base in Mount Zion.

This science fiction thriller is a tale of friendship, survival and sacrifice.
The story is set in the seven year tribulation, during the chaos following the sudden destruction.

Excerpt


Mei ran, pulling Liang with her. She scanned the crowd for Feng, Hai, Percy and the villagers she evacuated.

“Let’s go!” She jumped to compensate for her short stature, waving to get their attention.

They followed her out the emergency exit assigned to her group, running through winding paths towards her plane.

Glowing green eyes in the darkness ahead gripped her heart with terror. She knew those eyes. Ben!

Or rather, his superhuman, soulless replacement.

Backing off, she twirled her hand in an arc, signalling her group to turn around.

“I can smell you,” growled the inhuman creature, his lips parting in a brilliant white smile showcasing razor sharp fangs.

“Run,” she whispered. “Route twelve.” 

Mei stood her ground, determined to ensure that all in her charge make it to safety.

“We can’t lose you,” hissed Liang, inserting herself between Mei and the monster, nodding at Feng.

Grabbing her hand, Feng ran, pulling Mei along with him.

Ben raised his hand.
Green fire gushed out of his hand, striking Liang.
Incinerating her.

Liang!

Mei froze. Horrified.

Feng scooped Mei in his arms and sprinted, carrying her to her plane.

Their group had already boarded Mei’s plane.

Feng deposited Mei.
