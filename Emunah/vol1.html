Before Harpazo

By Janice Wee

The Calm Before the Snatching

The clock is ticking toward Harpazo—the moment when the faithful are snatched away. In Before Harpazo, the first volume of the Emunah Short Stories series, four lives unfold in the deceptive calm of the modern era. Love, faith, and destiny collide as unseen forces loom just beyond the horizon. These stories will grip your soul and leave you yearning for the rapture to come.

  • Abigail: Torn between a manipulative charmer and a loyal friend, her heart’s choice could seal her fate.
  • Oliver: A soul-winner hunted by a dark entity. Will he claim his glorious destiny before evil strikes?
  • Emunah: Secrets unravel as she teeters between romance and a supernatural threat. What lies within Beast Corp?
  • John: A frail bookworm must transform to save his mother. Can he lift the weight of his calling?

A storm loomed—but a stranger with mesmerizing eyes and a black umbrella changed everything. Who is he, and why does her heart race?
