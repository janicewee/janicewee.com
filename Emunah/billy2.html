Overview

Join Billy and Bluma in a thrilling new chapter of Janice Wee’s The Adventures of Billy Lionheart series!

Reunited at boarding school, the childhood friends stumble upon a mysterious gold coin bearing the likeness of Billy’s old nanny, Mathilda, and a cryptic treasure map. With Billy’s loyal lion guardian, Leonard, by their side, they sneak out of their dormitory to embark on a daring quest.

But danger lurks - a cunning trap separates them, locking Billy and Bluma in a dungeon while Leonard is shipped to a deadly game-hunting island.

Can the children outsmart their captors and save their beloved lion before he becomes a hunter’s trophy?

Perfect for middle-grade readers who crave fantasy, adventure, and heart-pounding suspense!
