Characters

Singapore's Runaway isn't just a story - it's a symphony of cultures, a celebration of diversity, and an exploration of what it means to find oneself while being part of a bigger whole. As you delve into the lives of these characters, you're invited to sail with them on this extraordinary journey, to witness their growth, their challenges, and their triumphs.

Welcome aboard, and let's set sail on this journey of discovery!

The Characters of Singapore's Runaway

Ah Cheng

Meet Ah Cheng, a young Chinese sailor hiding an explosive secret beneath his quiet exterior. Endowed with an uncanny mastery of martial arts, he conceals his skills like a shadow in the dark, fearing the risk it poses to his humble position on the ship.

Ahmad

Ahmad is the heartstring of Singapore's Runaway. A Singaporean Malay, this steadfast sailor's life is a rich tapestry woven with threads of love from a vast extended family stretching across Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

View Profile

Captain David Hawkins

Meet Captain David Hawkins, the formidable leader at the helm of the ship where Stanley seeks refuge in Singapore's Runaway. Captain Hawkins is a towering figure, his powerful presence filling every inch of the ship he commands. Yet, beneath his stern exterior lies a fatherly heart, beating with a profound affection for his crew.

View Profile

George Wong

Enter George, Stanley's relentless step-uncle, dispatched by his stepmother on a mission to scour the surrounding islands for the runaway youth in Singapore's Runaway. George is a man of singular focus, his determination matched only by the urgency of his task - to bring Stanley back to Singapore.

View Profile

Gopal

Introducing Gopal, the linguistic maestro and the ship's unofficial translator in Singapore's Runaway. A Singaporean Indian, he is the bridge between tongues, his fluency in English, Malay, Tamil, Hokkien, and Chinese a unique gift that unites the multicultural crew. He's a man of many words, but also a man of the people-always in good terms with everyone, his easy smiles and ready assistance making him a favorite among the crew. Though he possesses a keen business mind, Gopal's heart is rich with the treasure of friendship.

Officer Frederick Hardy

Step aboard and meet Officer Frederick Hardy, the stalwart right-hand man of Captain Hawkins in Singapore's Runaway. His stern, no-nonsense demeanor is as unwavering as the North Star, guiding the crew through the tumultuous seas of their journey.

Officer Joe Samuel

Though affable, Officer Joe Samuel's money-minded nature sometimes takes him into murky waters - he's not beneath selling out a shipmate for the right price. This ruthless pragmatism adds a layer of tension aboard the ship, his presence a wild card that could tilt the scales at any moment.

Officer Timmy Wright

Meet Officer Timmy Wright, the embodiment of mercy, brilliance, and justice on board the ship in Singapore's Runaway. His brilliant mind is a lighthouse, illuminating the shadows of deception to expose the real culprits, ensuring no individual is wrongly accused under his watch.

View Profile

Arnott Merritt

Introducing Arnott Merritt, the formidable leader of a faction of white crew members in Singapore's Runaway. Set during the British Colonial rule, Merritt is a living embodiment of the era's deeply ingrained belief in white privilege. A hulking figure, he uses his size and strength to assert dominance over the smaller Asian crew members. His actions mirror the larger currents of colonial power dynamics, his bullying a microcosm of the greater injustices of the period.

View Profile

Oswaldo

Meet Oswaldo, the shadow behind Arnott Merritt in Singapore's Runaway. As trustworthy as a coiled snake, Oswaldo's every move is a dance of deception. As Arnott's right-hand man, he paints a picture of loyalty, but behind this facade, he masterfully pulls the strings, subtly manipulating the leader to serve his own ends. Driven by an insatiable lust for the treasure hinted at in a missing map, Oswaldo's true intentions lie hidden beneath layers of cunning and deceit.

Ted Lawrence

Introducing Ted Lawrence, the third member of the formidable white trio led by Arnott in Singapore's Runaway. Lawrence, a man with a thirst for adventure and money, follows Arnott like a shadow, swept up in the tempest of their original mission. Yet, he finds himself at a crossroads when Arnott experiences a change of heart. His loyalty is tested, his path clouded with uncertainty as he is forced to choose between his former leader and their shared objective.

Tan Ah Kow

Say hello to Tan Ah Kow, a charismatic member of the multicultural crew on the ship in Singapore's Runaway. Ah Kow, a good friend of Stanley's, is a charming flirt who leaves a trail of swooning hearts in every port. Despite his roguish ways, his loyalty to his friends, especially Stanley, is as steady as the ship's course.

View Profile

Amelia Wong

As Stanley's stepmother, Amelia Wong's actions set the wheels of the narrative in motion. Her imposing presence at home becomes too overwhelming for Stanley, pushing him to seek refuge in the vastness of the sea. Yet, beneath her stern exterior, Amelia is a woman wracked with guilt. She sends her brothers on a relentless search across the seas to find Stanley and bring him back, her remorse driving her desperate quest for redemption.

View Profile

Stanley Tan

Meet Stanley, the Straits Born Chinese protagonist in Singapore's Runaway. Fleeing home upon the arrival of a new stepmother, Stanley embarks on a thrilling journey at sea. Despite his longing for his familiar life, pride and anger anchor him to his decision. Amidst the exhilarating waves of independence, he discovers the true essence of family through his crewmates. Stanley's adventure is a captivating saga of youthful rebellion, freedom, and a poignant quest for belonging.
