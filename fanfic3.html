Billy Batson is a Hero Fan Fics

My latest works are parked in Itty Bitty Billy One Shots in AO3, posted as Jam5, but these pick up where I left off in Super Reporter Billy Batson.

In my last story Captain Marvel was revealed to be itty bitty Billy Batson in Star City, while helping Green Arrow and Black Canary tackle a wacky foe.

Billy's so adoptable, Ollie and Dinah joined in the battle to claim the kid as their own. (Too bad Bruce.)

Billy takes to archery like a duck to water and he and Ollie drive Dinah up the wall. All this is going off canon. I don't know much about Green Arrow and Black Canary in DC Canon or the Arrowverse, but I take my inspiration from DC's graphic novel, Justice, to portray the relationship between Green Arrow and Black Canary.

Besides fan fiction, I write original stories with my own characters as well.
