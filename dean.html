Dean

At just three years old, Dean is a delightful bundle of energy and joy. He is often found trailing his elder sister, delighting in the outdoor activities and pranks orchestrated by his siblings.

Dean thrives in rough play, assuming the role of superheroes, engaging in playful fights with makeshift weapons, and navigating the rough and tumble of childhood with infectious laughter. Among all the siblings and cousins, Dean's speed is unmatched. His involvement in their adventures stems from both his relentless determination and his siblings' belief that he's safer accompanying them rather than being left alone.

As the youngest of the group, Dean is cherished by all, yet remains unspoiled. He displays a rare consideration for others at his tender age. Dean's appetite matches his boundless energy, eating just about anything placed before him. His energy ensures he burns off what he consumes just as quickly.

When the day's adventures have wound down and things grow quiet, Dean loves nothing more than to fall into a deep sleep, unless his curiosity prompts him to wander off on his own.
