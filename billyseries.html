A #Fantasy Adventure Series

Itty bitty little Billy Lionheart runs away from his foster home to find his real parents. He was a toddler when Mei found him in ruins - the only survivor after the massacre of his community by the Antichrist during the Tribulation. His parents, like the rest of the community, were tribulation saints. Mei, Boris, Ava and Leo took him into their home during the Millennial Kingdom of Christ. This story begins after Emunah Short Stories ends. It shifts from dystopian, to a world that's like the Garden of Eden, where mortals and immortals walk alongside one another. It is a world where man and animals live in harmony, and lions eat hay.

It's a fun filled fantasy adventure with twists and turns and lots of laugh out loud moments. The series is perfect for middle grade readers and those who love the lighthearted, feel-good stories.

Billy the Lion Boy

A #Fantasy Adventure for middle grade readers and the young at heart.

Billy knows this real parents have returned as immortals. He runs away from his foster home to look for them. His nanny, a lion named Leonard, who is equipped with a translator glove that allows him to speak all languages, accompanies the boy. They get lost and Leonard raises Billy as his human cub with the lion cubs he has with his mate, the lioness, Lina.

This is a heartwarming story about family, both found and biological, with hilarious moments as well as poignant ones.

Billy & Bluma. Double Trouble.

A thrilling fantasy adventure. A tale of friendship, mischief and forgiveness.

Billy and Bluma team up to find a long lost friend, while the unwittingly drive their lion guardian, Leonard, batty. Twists and turns make this a page turner that you wouldn't want to put down.
