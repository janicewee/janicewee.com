Captain David Hawkins

The Charismatic Leader

Introducing Captain David Hawkins, the charismatic and authoritative leader of The Everingham, a ship that sails through the intriguing world created by Janice Wee in her novel Singapore's Runaway. Born in 1880, Captain Hawkins is a British man with strong opinions and high principles. A natural leader and father figure to his crew, he guides them through their tumultuous journeys.

A former squaddy in the Royal Marines, Captain Hawkins possesses unmatched experience and tactical knowledge. He trains his crew for pirate attacks and selects them based on their physical strength and fighting abilities. His commitment to the safety and well-being of his crew is unwavering.

As the story unfolds, Captain Hawkins is impressed by Stanley, a young Chinese Singaporean man who saves his daughter from drowning in a stormy sea. Recognizing Stanley's strength and intelligence, he offers the young man a place in his crew. Stanley accepts the offer and proves to be a hardworking and resourceful member of the team. Captain Hawkins develops a soft spot for Stanley and becomes a father figure to him, while maintaining his fairness and justice at all times.

Driven by integrity and an unwavering commitment to his word, Captain Hawkins' ultimate goal is to ensure the safe and timely transport of cargo and sailors from one destination to another. His exceptional leadership and devotion to his crew make him a captivating character in Janice Wee's literary universe.
